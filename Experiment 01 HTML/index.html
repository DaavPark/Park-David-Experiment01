My First Internet Memory

Metal Fight Beyblade

I remember watching Metal Fight Beyblade online when I was young. I found episodes on random websites, and it felt like discovering a secret world. The action, colors, and battles stuck in my mind. It was my first time feeling the excitement of the internet.
